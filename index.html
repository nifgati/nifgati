If something said in the shiur hurt you

This space exists for anyone who felt hurt, dismissed, embarrassed, or unsettled by something said in the MDY Daf Yomi shiur.

You can describe what happened and what you wish had been said instead. Keep it respectful and focused on your experience.

Share what hurt

You may post anonymously. Please do not include private names, addresses, or identifying details.

If it helps, you can use this structure:

  • What was the topic?
  • What part hurt?
  • How did it affect you?
  • What would you have wanted to hear instead?

About this space

Nifgati is not a place to attack anyone. It is a space for those who felt hurt to be heard calmly and respectfully.

Respect

Focus on your experience. Avoid insults or threats.

Privacy

Do not share personal phone numbers, addresses, or identifying information.

Purpose

The goal is awareness and sensitivity, not hostility.
