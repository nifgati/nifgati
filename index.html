About this space

Nifgati exists for people who felt hurt by something said in the MDY Daf Yomi shiur by R’ Eli, and want a quiet place to put words to it.

Keep it respectful

Describe what landed for you and why. Avoid insults or threats.

Protect privacy

Don’t post names, phone numbers, addresses, or identifying details. Paraphrase rather than quoting exact lines.

What this is and isn’t

This is not a place to “take down” anyone. It’s a place to be heard, collect patterns, and encourage sensitivity going forward.
